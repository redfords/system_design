**SQL** How to do grouping, aggregation, use HAVING, windows, do a SUM on a CASE statement, and do inner / left / self joins. Be comfortable with subqueries and CTEs.

**Big O Notation** You need to know how to calculate time complexity, and one interview asked about space complexity. Disney+ also expected me to know the complexity of Python's .sort().

**Python** a ton of list and dictionary based questions. Be certain you can do most Leetcode Easy in 5 to 10 min and Medium in 30 min. I skipped a lot of binary tree problems, and linked list only came up once (Disney+).

**Kimball database design** came up in 2 to 3 interviews. I read Agile Data Warehouse Design and The Complete Reference: Star Schema.

- Specific Spark questions, things like "why would you use coalesce vs repartition". I wish I had read The Definitive Guide to Spark first
